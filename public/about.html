About Tees By Shelsea

Crafting premium quality clothing with passion and dedication since our inception

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Tees By Shelsea began with a simple vision: to provide high-quality, comfortable clothing that everyone can afford. What started as a small dream has grown into a trusted brand that serves both individual customers and wholesale partners across the country.

Our founder, Shelsea, recognized a gap in the market for premium quality clothing at accessible prices. With a background in fashion design and a passion for business, she launched Tees By Shelsea with just a few t-shirt designs and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, we're proud to offer over 500 products across multiple categories, serve 50+ wholesale partners, and have satisfied thousands of customers who trust our brand for their clothing needs.

Our Mission

To democratize fashion by making high-quality, stylish clothing accessible to everyone, while building lasting partnerships with retailers who share our commitment to customer satisfaction and ethical business practices.

We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel great in their clothes, regardless of their budget. That's why we work tirelessly to maintain the perfect balance between quality, style, and affordability in every product we create.

Our Core Values

These values guide every decision we make and every relationship we build

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every product undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets our high standards before reaching our customers.

Customer Centric

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We listen to feedback, adapt to needs, and constantly strive to exceed expectations.

Partnership

We build long-term relationships with our wholesale partners, supporting their growth and success as if it were our own business.

Innovation

We continuously innovate in design, materials, and processes to stay ahead of trends and deliver fresh, exciting products.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, using eco-friendly materials where possible and minimizing our environmental impact.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in every aspect of our business, from product design to customer service, wholesale support, and beyond.

Our Journey

2020
The Beginning
Shelsea launched Tees By Shelsea with 10 t-shirt designs and a vision to make quality clothing accessible to everyone.
2021
Product Expansion
Expanded our product line to include jeans, shoes, and accessories. Reached our first 100 customers milestone.
2022
Wholesale Launch
Launched our wholesale program with 5 initial partners. Introduced children's clothing line to serve entire families.
2023
Major Growth
Reached 25 wholesale partners and expanded to serve customers in 24 states. Launched our quality guarantee program.
2024
Innovation Focus
Introduced sustainable product lines and enhanced our online platform. Reached 50+ wholesale partners.
2025
Future Forward
Continuing to expand our product range and wholesale network while maintaining our commitment to quality and affordability.
