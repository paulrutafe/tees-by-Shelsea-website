Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping times?
We typically ship orders within 2-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. Wholesale orders may have different timelines depending on order size.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we ship within the United States only. However, we're working on expanding our shipping to international destinations. Please contact us for more information about international wholesale opportunities.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for retail customers. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. For wholesale returns, please contact your account manager to discuss the specific terms of your wholesale agreement.
How can I become a wholesale partner?
Getting started with our wholesale program is easy! Visit our wholesale page to learn about our different tiers and fill out the application form. Our sales team will review your application and contact you within 24 hours.
Do you offer custom branding for wholesale orders?
Yes! Enterprise tier wholesale partners have access to custom packaging and branding options. This includes custom labels, hang tags, and packaging design. Contact our wholesale team to discuss your specific branding needs.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our shipping partner's website. For wholesale orders, your account manager will provide detailed tracking information.
